Who we are?

A Motion Systems Company

Belle Vitesse provides specialized motion systems for film and commercial productions, covering the complete motion pipeline from camera vehicles to camera supports and remote heads.

Motion in Practice

We design, adapt, and supply premium electric camera platforms and precision motion systems, combining custom engineering with best-in-class existing equipment when appropriate. All systems are configured to deliver predictable, repeatable motion, with a strong emphasis on safe operation, controlled movement, and on-set reliability. Equipment can be delivered or picked up, and supplied with dedicated operators or pilots for the most demanding configurations.

Designed for Real-World Production

Our solutions are designed for real-world shooting conditions, including urban constraints, sound-critical environments, and sensitive locations, with a low-profile, quiet, and eco-friendly approach that ensures a discreet on-set footprint. Based in Paris, Belle Vitesse operates nationwide across France.
