Who we are?

Our mission

Belle Vitesse is a specialized motion equipment company dedicated to cinematic movement and precision camera motion for film and commercial productions. The company was created to address the growing demand for reliable, purpose-built motion systems, covering the entire motion pipeline from camera vehicles to camera supports and remote heads.

Our goals

Belle Vitesse designs, adapts, and supplies electric camera platforms, mounting solutions, and precision camera heads, combining custom engineering with carefully selected best-in-class existing equipment when appropriate. Our systems are chosen and configured for real-world shooting environments where urban constraints, safety, and efficiency are critical, offering a low-profile, quiet, and eco-friendly approach to motion work. Built through field experience and on-set requirements, each system is configured to deliver predictable, repeatable camera movement, while minimizing setup time and maintaining a discreet operational footprint. Our modular and adaptable configurations integrate seamlessly into professional camera workflows, allowing productions to move efficiently while maintaining control, reliability, and safety throughout the shoot. Based in Paris, Belle Vitesse operates nationwide across France.
